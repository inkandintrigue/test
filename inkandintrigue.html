Ink and Intrigue

Welcome to this intriguing enigma game!

The goal of the game is simple: to progress through different phases by entering the correct password. Each phase presents a unique enigma that will test your logic, creativity, and problem-solving skills. But beware, the answers are not always what they seem!

To proceed to the next level, you must correctly enter the password in all lowercase letters. Additionally, the passwords doesn't contain any type of ponctuation except for the interrogation point. Numbers are also admitted.

As you delve deeper into the game, the enigmas will become more challenging and intricate so I recomend that you take notes along the game. Whether you are a seasoned enigma solver or a newcomer to the world of puzzles, this game offers a stimulating and enjoyable experience for everyone. So, get ready to embark on this thrilling journey by starting to write the phrase "aM, I tHe...BesT? 8621" as the password, keeping in mind the password conditions.
